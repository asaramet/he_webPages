BW HPC Resources on bwUniCluster

Since January 2017, University of Applied Sciences Esslingen being a partner in bwHPC project, offers it's researchers access to bwUniCluster resources, a constantly updated HPC Cluster at KIT (Karlsruhe Institute of Technology).
